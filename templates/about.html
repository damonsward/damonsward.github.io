About Me

I matriculated in 2016 with an interest in IT and so I applied at Stellenbosch University for enrolment in a degree in Computer Science.

I studied a BSc in Mathematical Science in Computer Science at Stellenbosch University and graduated in December 2020. I studied various subjects like Applied Mathematics, Mathematics, Probability Theory and Statistics, Operations Research, Scientific Computing, Economics and of course Computer Science.

I am currently studying my BSc Honours in Computer Science at Stellenbosch University that is scheduled to be completed in December 2021.

My previous work experience was in the hospitality industry. I worked at Dutch Haven Bistro as a waiter and barista and at Rocomamas Willowbridge as a waiter. Working as a waiter helped me improve my communication skills and taught me to be organized because I worked part-time while studying.

My hobbies and interests include: creating websites and mobile applications, reading, finances and almost all sports.
